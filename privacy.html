Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 June 2025  ·  Last Updated: 1 June 2026  ·  Governing Law: Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India)

This Privacy Policy applies to all platforms, tools, and services operated by Real Plannet (MSME Registered, India), including but not limited to Career Blueprint, Wealth Compass, Digital Presence, and any future products or services offered under the Real Plannet brand at realplannet.com and its subdomains.

1. Who We Are

Real Plannet is a registered Micro, Small and Medium Enterprise (MSME) incorporated in India. We design, build, and deliver simplified digital tools across career planning, wealth management, digital presence, and related domains. Our registered contact for data matters is hello@realplannet.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact and identity data: Name, email address, phone number — collected when you submit enquiry forms or contact us
  • Service-specific inputs: Career goals, financial preferences, business details, or other information you enter into our platforms to receive AI-assisted outputs
  • Communication data: Messages, feedback, or support requests you send to us

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns — collected via Google Analytics 4
  • Device and browser data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL
  • Cookies and similar technologies: Session cookies and analytics cookies — see Section 8 for details

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect Aadhaar numbers, PAN details, financial account credentials, biometric data, or any sensitive personal data as defined under the SPDI Rules, 2011, unless explicitly required for a specific service and separately consented to.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver, personalise, and improve our platforms and services
  • To process your service enquiries and respond to communications
  • To generate AI-assisted outputs based on the inputs you provide
  • To analyse platform usage and improve user experience
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations
  • To send service-related communications (not marketing, unless separately opted-in)

AI-Generated Outputs: Several Real Plannet platforms use artificial intelligence to process your inputs and generate personalised results. The information you provide to generate outputs is processed solely for that purpose. We do not use your inputs to train AI models, sell data to third parties, or profile you for advertising purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Consent: Where you have provided explicit consent — e.g., submitting a contact form or using a platform feature
  • Contractual necessity: To deliver a service you have requested
  • Legitimate interests: For platform analytics and security, where such interests are not overridden by your rights
  • Legal obligation: Where processing is required under applicable Indian law

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Third-party tools that help us operate our platforms (Google Analytics, Web3Forms, Cloudflare). These providers are contractually bound to process data only as instructed.
  • Legal authorities: Where required by law, court order, or governmental authority in India
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to equivalent privacy protections

Third-Party Services in Use

  • Google Analytics 4 — usage analytics (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Web3Forms — form submission processing (governed by Web3Forms' Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare — content delivery and security (governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery via Google CDN

6. Your Rights Under DPDPA 2023

As a Data Principal under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a summary of personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Grievance redressal: File a complaint with our Grievance Officer (see Section 11)
  • Nomination: Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time — this will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal

To exercise any of these rights, write to us at hello@realplannet.com with the subject line "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law:

  • Contact form submissions: 12 months from the date of submission
  • Platform usage data (analytics): 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Service records: Duration of engagement plus 3 years, as required under Indian commercial law

Upon expiry of the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

8. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies on our platforms:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 cookies to understand how visitors use our platforms. These are only activated with your consent where required by law.

You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling analytics cookies will not affect your ability to use our platforms.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse, in accordance with the IT (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011. These include encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), restricted access controls, and regular review of our security practices.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to affect your rights, we will notify you and the relevant authority as required under applicable law.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platforms are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided personal data without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a minor has submitted data to us, please contact us at hello@realplannet.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our services. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any revisions. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our platforms after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
